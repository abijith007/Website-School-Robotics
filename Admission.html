Admission Details
Any students interesested to join Robotech School can join by writing the entrance exam. A sample test is present in this wwebsite to check the childs performance, which would then help realise students to realise the areas needed to be improved and thus excell in the admission test.

Score Rate
1. 0-3 marks-The student has to improve on his general knowledge, analytical and reasoning abilities and need to work hard.
2. 4-7 marks=The student has good knowledge but needs to improve his analytical and reasoning abilities.
3. 8-10 marks-The student has excellent knowledge and has good general knowledge, analytical and reasoning abilities there are high chances of the student to get admission.
